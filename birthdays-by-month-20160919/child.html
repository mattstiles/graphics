Average Births Per Day: 1994-2014

Over the last two decades, each calendar day has averaged between 6,500 and 12,300 births. The average is 11,131 per day. Daily birth totals drop on many holidays (and spike on Valentine’s Day).

Data: U.S. National Center for Health Statistics (1994-2003); U.S. Social Security Administration (2004-2014) — via FiveThirthyEight
