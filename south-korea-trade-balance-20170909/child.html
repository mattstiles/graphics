South Korea’s Global Trade Balance

South Korea’s balance of trade, or its exports minus its imports, varies widely by country, both in raw dollars and as a percentage its overall trade with various nations. These charts show the latter measure, by year, with the world’s largest economies. Green bars reflect a positive trade balance for South Korea. Orange bars mean it suffered a trade deficit with a particular country in a given year.

Notes

Figures normalized to reflect the trade balance as a percentage of overall trade in a given year.

Source: Korea Customs Service
