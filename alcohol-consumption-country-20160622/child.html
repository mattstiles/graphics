Daily Alcohol Intake, By Country

The World Health Organization surveyed people around the world who consider themselves “drinkers”. The average daily intake in grams of “pure alcohol” per day varied widely by country. In some places, a greater proportion of men reported drinking — and in varying amounts — skewing the average totals attributed to “both sexes” by the organization.

Notes

Data represent average daily intake of alcohol in 2010 among people who reported themselves as “drinkers”. View the metadata here.
