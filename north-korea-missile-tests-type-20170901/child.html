North Korea’s Missile Tests: 1984-2017

The nation has tested at least 115 missiles, including the August 28 test of a Hwasong-12 missile from Pyongyang Airport. At least 26 “failed”.

Notes

Failure is a catastrophic event during the flight. For example, a missile exploding.
