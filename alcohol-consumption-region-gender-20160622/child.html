Daily Alcohol Intake, By World Region

The World Health Organization surveyed people around the world who consider themselves “drinkers”. The average daily intake in grams of “pure alcohol” per day varied widely by region. In some regions, a greater proportion of men reported drinking — and in varying amounts — skewing the average totals attributed to “both sexes” by the organization. Explore the data below.
