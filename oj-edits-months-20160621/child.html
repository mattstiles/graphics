O.J. Wikipedia Edits, By Month: 2003-2016

Wikipedia users have edited O.J. Simpson’s entry more than 4,000 times since it began in 2003. The changes were particularly frequent in 2006, after the controversial “If I Did It” book project. Edits also spiked in September 2007, when Simpson was arrested in Las Vegas on armed robbery charges.
